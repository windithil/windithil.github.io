HOME

Welcome to my website.

I am an engineer with a passion for design and creation.

I have a MSc in flight dynamics & control, and a BSc in aerospace engineering from sharif university of technology.

I have made this website through experimenting with threejs Javascript library and webgl renderer, thanks to the teachings of Bruno Simon and Yuri Artiukh.

RECENT YEARS

I conduct academic research in the field of active space debris removal. My work is focused on a specific debri removal method based on flexible momentum exchange principle. The work heavily relies on numerical simulation of the dynamics using Matlab.
Over the last couple of years my learning path has drifted towards expanding my coding skills.
I have explored programming languages like python and Javascript. I also touched on the topic of web3 and gained some insight into solidity.
My most recent adventure is CH3SS as one of the co-founders of the first fully decentralized chess protocol.

YOUTH

I was fond of engineering and I had the utmost awe for crafts that flew in the air.
Fortunately I was accepted to aerospace engineering program at Sharif university of technology and found the oppurtunity to earn bachelor's degree with the focus on airplane design, concluding it by publishing a paper presenting the design of an unmanned exploration plane for martian atmosphere.

Later, I joined the graduate program and earned a master's degree in controls and flight dynamics. I did my thesis on dynamic identification of a coanda effect air vehicle and published the findings in a paper, making the cover of IEEE Aerospace and Electronic Systems Magazine.

CHILDHOOD

I was a curious soul from early years. I was seriously interested in things that worked.

My early teenage years were spent reading through a series of books by Isaac Asimov on history of various scientific fields. The experience somehow shaped my life as I was captivated by the life and efforts of scientists.

I spent most of my spare time crafting gadgets I aspired to be inventions and exploring different methods of destroying my toys!

Later, in high school I started making robots with my friends and participated half a dozen robotic competitions. We even earned a runner up title in university tier event with a fire-fighter robot.
